Bio

Hello, I'm Phillip. You'll know me as DonutDeflector online. I'm a hobbyist programmer, web developer, and computer nerd. I also enjoy playing the piano, drawing, and watching anime.

Web Development

My main focus is Front-End Web Developement. The combination of graphic design and logical problem solving really appeal to me. My approach to web design is KISS: Keep It Simple, Stupid. I aim to build fast, slim, and easy-to-use sites. Not everyone has access to fast internet and speedy hardware. I would like to allow these users to access my webpages quickly and without frustration.

Programming

I do a bit of programming on the side. I've programmed in C, C#, Javascript, Python, and Shell. My experience with C, C#, and Python is minimal. The bulk of my time is spent with HTML, CSS, and Shell.

Linux

I've been using Linux since 2014. Currently, my laptop runs Manjaro with XFCE and my desktop runs Arch with i3wm. My Raspberry Pi runs Rasbian and acts as a git server, seedbox, and RTMP server.

Music

I've been taking piano lessons since 2012. I'd estimate my raw playing skill level to be somewhere around level 4 or 5 ABRSM. In the realm of theory, I know the basics. I've made a few compositions. I've played classical, contemporary, and a small bit of ragtime. I also mess around with the recorder and harmonica. You'll frequently hear me playing some sort of instrument in the high school cafeteria.

Drawing

I'm alright at drawing, I guess. People say I draw pretty well, so judge me however you want. I really don't have much formal training other than art class in school. It's a fun thing to do and get good at. I draw a mix of manga and semi-realistic observation stuff.

Anime

Anime is my favorite film medium. Lots of great stuff comes out Japan and East Asia. I'm particulary attracted to 2D animation and storytelling that is offered by the medium. Actually, I keep a list of these Japanese cartoons.
